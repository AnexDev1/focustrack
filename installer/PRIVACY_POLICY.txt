FocusTrack Privacy Notice

Last updated: March 10, 2026

FocusTrack is a privacy-first desktop application. The application stores screen time activity, app usage summaries, and related analytics locally on your device so that you can review your own productivity data.

By default, FocusTrack does not require an account and does not upload your activity history to FocusTrack-operated servers.

FocusTrack may locally store:
- Active app names and window titles
- Session timestamps and durations
- Category totals, streaks, analytics, and preferences

FocusTrack does not, by default:
- Log keystrokes
- Capture clipboard contents
- Sell your data
- Embed third-party ads

Export features may create JSON or CSV files on your device when you choose to export your data.

If you distribute FocusTrack publicly, replace any placeholder contact or support information with your own.
